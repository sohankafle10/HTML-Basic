Introduction

Welcome to lumbini ICT, where academic excellence meets vibrant campus life! Situated in the heart of a Gaindakot Municipality,Nawalparasi. Lumbini ICT Campus is renowned for its commitment to providing students with a well-rounded education that prepares them for success in their chosen fields. At Lumbini ICT, we pride ourselves on our diverse and inclusive community, where students from all walks of life come together to learn, grow, and thrive. Our dedicated faculty members are experts in their fields, passionate about teaching, and committed to helping students reach their full potential. With a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs to choose from, including Management, Computer sciences, and more, Lumbini ICT offers something for everyone. Our state-of-the-computer facilities, research opportunities, and robust extracurricular activities ensure that students have access to all the resources they need to excel both inside and outside the classroom. Whether you're interested in pursuing a traditional four-year degree, participating in study abroad programs, or engaging in hands-on internships, Lumbini ICT Campus provides endless opportunities for personal and professional growth. Join us at Lumbini ICT Campus and embark on an unforgettable journey of discovery, learning, and achievement!
